# Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) Policy

## 1. Introduction
At **FyndBae**, we are committed to maintaining a safe and secure environment for all users. This policy outlines our strict stance against **Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)** and details the measures we take to prevent, detect, and report any such activities on our platform.

## 2. Zero-Tolerance Policy
**FyndBae** has a **zero-tolerance policy** for CSAE. Any user engaging in, promoting, or facilitating such activities will face immediate account suspension, law enforcement reporting, and permanent removal from our platform.

## 3. Prohibited Content and Activities
The following actions are strictly prohibited on **FyndBae**:
- Sharing, uploading, or distributing any content involving **child sexual abuse or exploitation**.
- Soliciting minors for any form of sexual activity.
- Attempting to communicate with minors in a manner deemed inappropriate or harmful.
- Using the platform to arrange, promote, or facilitate CSAE in any form.

## 4. Reporting and Moderation
We take CSAE reports seriously and act promptly upon receiving any such allegations. Our response includes:
- **Immediate Investigation**: Our moderation team reviews and investigates reports swiftly.
- **Law Enforcement Collaboration**: We report confirmed cases to the relevant authorities.
- **User Account Actions**: Offending accounts are permanently banned.

If you come across any CSAE-related content or activity, please report it immediately by contacting **fyndbae5@gmail.com**.

## 5. Preventive Measures
To safeguard our platform from CSAE, we implement:
- **Strict Age Verification**: Users must be **18 years or older** to access **FyndBae**.
- **AI and Manual Moderation**: We employ **automated detection tools** and **human moderators** to review flagged content.
- **User Education**: We provide guidelines on safe interactions and identifying suspicious behavior.
